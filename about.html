About TaskMate

TaskMate was built to help you organize tasks, focus on what matters, and collaborate with your team. We combine a clean UI with thoughtful features like focus sessions, progress tracking, and simple task management.

Our mission

To empower people and teams to get more meaningful work done with less friction — one focused session at a time.

Core values

  • Clarity — keep things simple and focused
  • Reliability — build tools you can trust
  • Respect — help users protect their time and attention

Who is TaskMate for?

Students, developers, professionals, and small teams who want a lightweight workflow to track tasks and protect focused time.
